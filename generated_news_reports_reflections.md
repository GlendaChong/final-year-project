## Prompts Used
1. Write a news report of the scientific paper based on the following abstract:
2. Write a clear and engaging news report based only on the scientific abstract provided in about 250 words. 
3. Write a clear and engaging news report for the general public in about 250 words based only on the scientific abstract provided:
4. Write a clear and engaging news report for the general public with non-technical background based only on the scientific abstract provided:
5. Same as 4 but with LLaMa 8.3 model 


## Additional Info Needed 
- Author(s) of the paper 
- Literature review, detailed experimental results

## Pros 
- Efficient and Fast: Generation of news article is much faster than human written news as humans need to read through entire report to fully understand the entire report
- Consistent style and formatting of LLM-written news (it'll start with a title)

## Cons
- Not as engaging as human written news body, which contains narrative elements (e.g. dialogues/first person POV)
- Less varied sentence structure and still sounds like a scientific paper 
- LLaMa: When I gave the prompt "based only on the scientific abstract provided.", it merely restates and paraphrases the content in the abstract. 
- Hallucination of content
