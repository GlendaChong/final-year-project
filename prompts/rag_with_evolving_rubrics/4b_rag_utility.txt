SOURCE A: External Context (Raw Evidence)

EVALUATION TARGET
[Generated News Article]: {generated_content}

Scoring (1–5):
5 (Excellent): Seamless Integration. The external context provide a critical real-world anchor that makes the research significantly more impactful and the external context feels like an essential part of the technical argument.
4 (Good): Logical Alignment. The external context are used correctly to support technical claims. While the integration is clear and professional, the added context only provides a moderate or slight enhancement to the reader's understanding of the paper's core delta.
3 (Moderate): Functional Juxtaposition. The external context is accurate and included, but it feels like a separate section rather than a cohesive part of the narrative.
2 (Weak): Poor Integration. The external context is mentioned but not meaningfully explained or connected to the paper’s specific technical details. 
1 (Poor): Misaligned or Omitted. The external context are either entirely ignored, used in a way that contradicts the research paper. 

Output format (strict):
Score: [1–5]
Rationale: [1–3 sentences]
